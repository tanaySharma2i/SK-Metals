About S.K. Metal

Established in 1993, S.K. Metal is a reputed steel fabrication vendor specializing in tensile structures, lightweight structures, interiors, exhibitions, and architectural metalwork.

Based out of our private workshop in Noida, U.P., we have successfully delivered fabrication projects across India and internationally — including in the UK, USA, and Copenhagen.

We provide end-to-end solutions including design detailing, fabrication, and onsite installation, with a focus on quality, reliability, and innovation.
